Site Plan - Pedro Castro

Site Name: Pedro Castro's Portfolio

I have chosen this name because it has my name and the word portfolio which indicates that the site is suposed to demonstrate what I can do. The title is concise and objective.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this portfolio website is to serve as a professional platform for showcasing my skills, expertise, and career aspirations. It will provide visitors, including potential employers and collaborators, with information about my background, technical and soft skills, and ways to connect with me. The site will include dynamic visualizations to demonstrate my capabilities and a contact page for easy communication. It aims to present me as a qualified and skilled candidate in the software development field.

Scenarios

Who is this person, and what are their skills?

A site visitor, such as a potential employer or recruiter, navigates to the website to learn about me. They want to quickly understand my background, technical expertise, and the value I can bring to their organization. The Home page will provide a concise introduction, while the Skills page will offer a detailed overview of my capabilities with dynamic visualizations and examples.

How can I get in touch with this candidate or view their work online?

A hiring manager or collaborator is interested in reaching out to me or reviewing more of my work. They navigate to the Contact page to find a form for direct communication and links to my professional profiles, such as LinkedIn and GitHub, for additional information about my projects and experience.
